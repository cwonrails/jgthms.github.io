September 2015

Barn d'Or Award

barn-dor.com

Reddit user /u/trainstobrazil decided to create a new award that would celebrate all those wonderful "Couldn't hit a barn door" moments in football.

I decided to provide a simple interface where one can easily navigate through all Barn d'Or contenders.
